Super Heros

Our greatest glory is not in never falling, but rising everytime we fall.-Batman

Superman

Superhuman strength, super speed, stamina and invulnerability, freezing breath, super hearing, multiple extrasensory and vision powers, longevity, flight, and regeneration.

Batman

A ruthless vigilante who frequently killed or maimed criminals, but evolved into a character with a stringent moral code and strong sense of justice.

Black Adam

The Egyptian Gods that empower Black Adam are: Shu, who grants him incredible stamina and invulnerability. Heru, who grants him unmatchable super speed. Amon, who grants him tremendous physical strength.
